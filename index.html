    About Us

    The old ways of applying to Universities are over. With Application Finder, students can easily look up what University or Course they want to apply to. Representatives or Admins from Universities can also manage students' Applications, as well as add new Programmes or edit existing ones.

    Our Mission

    Make applying to Universities much easier for students, and aid Universities in managing Students' Applications.

    Our Plan

    To expand and make this Website Service available worldwide so that all Students and Universities around the world can benefit from it.

    Our Vision

    To provide the tools in building a better learning environment for both Students and Universities so that both may achieve their goals.
